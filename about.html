FAST Update Generator

An internal tool for Head Start Aviation's Field Aviation Support Team

FAST Update Generator helps aircraft maintenance engineers at Head Start Aviation — a Gulfstream Authorized Service Provider — manage and communicate the status of on-site maintenance interventions (AOG support) around the world.

What it does

  • Generates structured, standardized status update reports for customers during an active maintenance job
  • Prepares draft emails (customer updates, parts requests, travel requests, FBO coordination) for the engineer to review and send
  • Extracts data from work order documents to speed up reporting
  • Tracks daily progress and parts used during an intervention

Who uses it

This application is used exclusively by Head Start Aviation's internal FAST engineering team. It is not available to the public and has no consumer-facing use case.
