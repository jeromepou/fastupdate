✈ FAST Update Generator — Help

🗂 How it works

  1. Create your profile (login screen) — your name, team, phone, email + a 4-digit PIN. This is YOUR profile as the app user. All your projects are stored here.
  2. Engineer profiles (⚙️ Settings → Engineer profiles) — add all the FAST engineers you work with. These are reusable across all your projects.
  3. Step 3 Condition — add the work items from the RFS. These stay fixed throughout the chantier.
  4. Step 4 Daily updates — every day, click "+ Today's update". For each work item, check the box, set its status, and write what was done. Add a global note at the bottom.
  5. Step 6 — select the engineers onsite from your saved profiles. Their info fills automatically.
  6. Generate → table in the exact FAST email format → Copy → Paste into email.
  7. Mark A/C RTS when done. The project is archived.

💾 Data storage

All data is stored locally in your browser (localStorage). Nothing is sent to any server. PIN protects your profile from casual access but is not bank-grade security.
